Wordle Clone

This is my clone of the popular game Wordle.

Your objective is to... guess the 5-letter word!

You have 6 chances to guess the word.

After you submit a word by pressing ENTER,
The tiles will change to one of three colors:

GRAY

The letter is NOT in the word!

YELLOW

The letter is in the word, but NOT in that spot!

GREEN

The letter is in the exact same spot as in the answer!
